GOLDEY IS A DJ
FROM MIDCOAST MAINE

Goldey, aka Aly, began DJing when the pandemic hit, at a time when going out and dancing to the music they loved was no longer possible. As a psychologist with a deep understanding of how electronic music supports wellbeing, Goldey curates dance-forward house sets with Latin and instrumental influences. They love organizing experiences that are designed to help people move, feel good, and promote connection.

SERVICES

DJ SETS

Music for dance parties of all kinds. From clubs and bars, to house parties for your friends or community fundraisers, they love to facilitate dance and create a vibe through music.

EVENT PRODUCTION

Want to throw a dance party, fundraiser, or class? They can help you make it happen from finding a location, to marketing, to producing it. If your fundraiser event is supporting queer folks, they're often happy to contribute the music and sound as part of that effort.

GEAR RENTAL

Offering sound system or mic rentals for parties, events, and small performances. Basic set-up available; two high-quality PA speakers, subwoofer, mixer, bluetooth microphone and corded microphone. Also a set of smaller Edifier speakers for more intimate spaces.

MUSIC

Goldey settles at the intersection of house, Latin, and instrumental dance music, with an emphasis on rhythm, warmth, and momentum. They're thinking about what keeps a dance floor feeling accessible and alive so that people lean into the benefits of dance.
